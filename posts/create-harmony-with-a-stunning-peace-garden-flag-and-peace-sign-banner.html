Care Tips for Your Peace Garden Flag

  • Gentle Cleaning: To keep your Peace Garden Flag, Peace Sign Banner looking vibrant, wash it with mild soap and lukewarm water. Avoid using bleach or harsh detergents that can damage the fabric. A soft cloth or sponge can help gently remove dirt and stains.
  • Proper Storage: When the seasons change or you plan to take a break from displaying your flag, fold it neatly and store it in a cool, dry place. Avoid damp areas that could promote mildew, and ensure it’s free from sharp objects that might cause tears.
  • Handle with Care: When installing or removing your flag from its pole, handle it gently to prevent wear and tear on the fabric. Try to avoid pulling or tugging on the edges, as this can lead to fraying over time.
  • Regular Inspection: Check your flag periodically for any signs of wear, such as fading or fraying. If you notice any minor damages, addressing them early can help prolong its life and keep the peace sign radiating charm in your garden.
