To make sure your new garden flag stays vibrant and beautiful, start by positioning it out of direct, harsh sunlight whenever possible. Cleaning is also key to longevity. Every so often, give it a gentle wash with mild soap and water to remove dirt and grime that can accumulate. Just make sure to air dry it completely before putting it back outside.
